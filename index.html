Our mission

Hello, we are the HHP - Helping Hopeless Programmers organisation. We started our operation on 12th of November 2025, and we are not stopping until all programmers regain motivation. Our mission is raising concern amongst humanity that a programmer's mental health should not depend on whether the code compiles. Through this organisation we want programmers (with and without jobs) to receive gifts and hopefully regain motivation for pursuing a career in this field.

Follow the instructions. Scroll down or click here, then select the products you would like to enlighten a programmer's life with.
